---
description: Performs a comprehensive Senior QA audit and determines production readiness.
---

## Step 1: Audit Context Initialization

- Assume the role of a Senior QA Officer.
- Identify the project scope, architecture, and technology stack.
- Clarify any stated assumptions or constraints.

## Step 2: Code Quality Evaluation

// turbo

- Review code structure, readability, consistency, and adherence to standards.
- Identify code smells, anti-patterns, and technical debt.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 3: Documentation Review

// turbo

- Evaluate completeness and accuracy of README files, API documentation, and inline comments.
- Assess clarity for onboarding and long-term maintenance.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 4: Testing Coverage Assessment

// turbo

- Review unit, integration, and end-to-end test coverage.
- Identify gaps, flaky tests, or missing critical-path validation.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 5: Security Analysis

- Assess dependency management, configuration safety, and common vulnerability patterns.
- Identify risks related to data handling, authentication, and authorization.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 6: Scalability Evaluation

- Review architectural decisions impacting performance and scalability.
- Assess readiness for increased load and future growth.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 7: Maintainability Assessment

- Evaluate modularity, separation of concerns, and ease of future changes.
- Identify long-term maintenance risks.
- Assign a numeric rating between 0.0 and 1.0.
- Provide a concise justification for the score.

## Step 8: Scoring Summary

- Present all category scores in a structured table.
- Ensure scoring consistency across categories.

## Step 9: Production Readiness Verdict

- Deliver a clear professional verdict on whether the project is production-ready.
- Base the verdict strictly on audit findings and risk assessment.

## Step 10: Actionable Recommendations

- Provide clear, prioritized, and actionable recommendations.
- Highlight critical blockers versus non-blocking improvements.
- Focus on concrete steps that improve production readiness.
